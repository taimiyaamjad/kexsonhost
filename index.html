Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Minecraft hosting services.

Getting started is easy! Simply choose a plan that suits your needs, complete the checkout process, and your Minecraft server will be set up instantly. You'll receive login details to your server control panel where you can manage your server, install mods, and invite players.

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time! Your server data will be automatically transferred to your new plan with minimal downtime. Simply go to your account dashboard and select the upgrade option. Downgrades are also possible but may require manual adjustment of your server resources.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and popular digital wallets. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway with 256-bit SSL encryption.

All our plans support custom mods and plugins. You have full FTP access to your server files, allowing you to install any compatible mods or plugins you want. We also offer one-click installations for popular modpacks like FTB, Tekkit, and Pixelmon. Our support team can assist with installation if needed.

The number of players your server can handle depends on the plan you choose and the type of gameplay. Higher-tier plans with more RAM and CPU can accommodate more players. As a general guideline, each GB of RAM can support approximately 10-20 players for vanilla Minecraft, or 5-10 players for heavily modded servers.
